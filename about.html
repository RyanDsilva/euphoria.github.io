Viva La Vida - Life Your Life!

As Maria Robinson once said, “Nobody can go back and start a new beginning, but anyone can start today and make a new ending” The average life span of an Human is a short 68 years so if we are going to live life to the fullest we better start today. You take in about 28,800 breathes in one day so why don't you go out and create moments that take those breaths away because I mean we have a few to spare. These four years are going to be the last four years of your college life so enjoy it to the fullest. With this we the student council present to you the theme of this year's euphoria
